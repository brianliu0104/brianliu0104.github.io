Running X

Running X provides a community platform that combines the traditional running APP's logging and social platform, allowing people to plan paths and invite friends to build groups, and to search for groups that you want to attend, and even to enjoy your own time.

We are convinced that "people who do not like to run, who can meet new friends or run with friends to improve their feelings, they will be more willing to go out of the house, to find a group of like-minded running friends in the promotion of good health while also make friends."

Hope that the future in the community part of the design can be more perfect, so that Running X become a running as the core of the social platform.

Interface

  • Create Team

    1.Create a group, group name and description

    2.Choose the date and the group photo

    3.Set up a path (Google Map)

    4.Privacy settings (private and public)

    5.Invite friends

  • Google Map

    1.Show nearby friends (photo)

    2.Updated every 5 seconds (power saving)

    3.Follow the path you planned

    4.Understand each other's status

    5.Racing mode

  • Control Your Speed

    1.Set the speed control (above the red and green line)

    2.Select the control rate (green: safe interval; red: slow speed)

    3.Beyond the critical interval (green) to remind the vibration way

    4.Record the average speed and statistical chart

    5.Instant start with pause button (Start Stop)
